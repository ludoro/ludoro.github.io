You can find my CV [here](/assets/LudovicoBessi.pdf).

I am currently a second (last) year Master degree student in Applied Mathematics with a focus on Machine learning and Data science at Politecnico di Torino, Italy.

A few things I am proud of in no particular order:

- From July 2021 until December 2021 I will be working at CERN, focusing on simulation techniques in Python to investigate the loss of landau damping thresholds for dipole and quadrupole oscillation modes and its dependence on initial parameters.

- I did my master degree thesis at Volvo cars from January 2021 to June 2021, I focused on computer vision models for object detection to support the Data and diagnostics team.

- I have worked under a NumFocus grant for the Alan Turing institute between September 2020 and December 2020, working mainly on epidemiological models in Julia and multi objective optimization algorithms related to fairness.

- I have worked under the Google summer of code grant between May 2020 and September 2020, keeping up the development of Surrogates.jl.

- I have been part of the Vodafone IoT Academy from February to August 2020, where as a group we analyzed a data set for an insurance company to segment the car drivers.

- I have worked 10 months as Machine Learning engineer intern at ClearBox.ai, I worked on Adversarial attacks, that is: looking for minimal perturbations that deceive existing machine learning models, with a focus on Computer vision applications.

- Between May 2019 and September 2019 I have developed Surrogates.jl, a new Julia library that deals with approximations of solution of differential equations. It entails sampling, surrogates and optimization methods. You can even take derivatives/gradients of such surrogates using Automatic differentiability. I find it pretty cool, that’s why I am writing a paper on that.

- Between November 2018 and November 2019 I have been part of “Policumbent”. I was in charge of writing Matlab simulations to improve the aerodynamic of the bike.

- In my bachelor thesis I worked at matrix vector code optimization.

I can write code in several programming languages, I especially like Python and Julia.

I am proficient in high level mathematical machinery such as: Functional Analysis, Numerical optimization, Measure based probability and statistics. I am fond of algorithmic theory as well.

My expertise is at the intersection of Mathematics, Simulation processes and Statistics. This combination of skills let me work in a wide range of projects. I really like this.
